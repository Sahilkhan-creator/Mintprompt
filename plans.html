Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you take a commission on sales?

No. You keep 100% of your revenue. We earn through subscription fees only.

Q: Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage your subscription directly through Gumroad.

Q: What happens to my products if I cancel?

Products remain live, but you won't be able to upload new ones until you resubscribe.

Q: Are sales guaranteed?

No. MintPrompt provides visibility and exposure, not guaranteed conversions.

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade plans?

Yes. Contact us via WhatsApp and we'll adjust your plan.

Q: What if my product gets rejected?

We'll provide feedback and allow resubmission if it's a quality issue, not spam.
